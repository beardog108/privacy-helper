Tips

Some measures are important to any threat model:

  • Enabling 2-factor authentication (not SMS if possible)
  • Keeping devices & software up to date
  • Not opening untrusted software
  • Understanding and listening to security warnings (certificate warnings, antivirus detections, phishing warnings)
  • Using good, unique passwords. Being mindful of username reuse (for privacy reasons)
